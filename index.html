Who We Are

POSA Pharmacy & My Health and Drug Consult (MHDC) is a professional healthcare and educational consultancy dedicated to improving lives through expert advice, consultation, and high-quality services.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of integrated healthcare and educational solutions, fostering health, knowledge, and professional growth.

Our Values

  • Integrity and professionalism
  • Excellence in service delivery
  • Client and student-centered approach
  • Continuous learning and growth

POSA Pharmacy Services

  • Prescription Dispensing: Fast and accurate filling of your prescriptions by licensed pharmacists.
  • Over-the-Counter Medication: Wide variety of non-prescription medicines and health products.
  • Medication Counseling: Expert advice on proper medication use and management.
  • Health Screenings: Routine blood pressure, glucose, and cholesterol checks.
  • Vaccination Services: Immunizations to protect you and your family.
  • Home Delivery: Convenient delivery of medications to your doorstep.

MHDC Services

  • Academic and Education Consultation: We assist students with academic planning and choosing the right course for a successful educational journey.
  • Admission Consultation For Students: We guide students through the admission process into schools, colleges, and universities.
  • Career Coaching: Helps individuals discover their strengths, plan professional paths, and achieve career success.
  • Job Placement Services: We connect qualified individuals with job opportunities, especially in the healthcare sector.
  • Health And Drug Workshop Training: Workshops on health education, drug safety, and awareness.
  • Training For Pharmacists, Clinics, and PPMVs: Specialized training to improve healthcare delivery and business management.
  • Leadership and Mentoring: Mentoring to develop leadership skills, confidence, and professional growth.
  • Healthcare Consultation: Expert advice to help individuals understand their health concerns.
  • Drug Consultation: Guidance on safe use of medicines, including dosages and side effects.
  • Consultation For Colleges of Health Sciences: Advisory services on training and curriculum development.

Meet The Owner

PHARM OKE SILAS ADEYEMI

As the visionary CEO of POSA PHARMACY and MY HEALTH AND DRUG CONSULT (MHDC), Pharm Oke Silas Adeyemi is a respected leader in healthcare service delivery. He is committed to excellence and has built a reputation for leadership, innovation, and mentorship.

LEADERSHIP AND MENTORSHIP: Pharm Oke is dedicated to nurturing the next generation of healthcare professionals. He provides leadership, mentoring, and consultation services to students, empowering them with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to excel in their careers. His guidance and expertise have been invaluable to many students, helping them navigate the complexities of the healthcare industry and providing them with job coaching and job placements that have helped many.He has a proven track record of mentoring others into becoming a good healthcare service provider.

CONSULTING SERVICES: In addition to his leadership roles, Pharm Oke offers consulting services to colleges of health, providing expert advice and support to students, faculty, and administrators most especially on admission increase , providing consultation on accreditation and indexing . His consulting services focus on curriculum development, program evaluation, and strategic planning, helping institutions achieve their goals and improve outcomes.

COMMITMENT TO EXCELLENCE: Through his work at POSA PHARMACY and MY HEALTH AND DRUG CONSULT, Pharm Oke remains committed to delivering exceptional healthcare services, promoting wellness, and advancing the healthcare industry. His leadership, mentorship, and consulting services have made a lasting impact on the lives of many students, healthcare professionals, and patients.He is building something spectacular in the area of providing quality pharmaceuticals, consulting services for College of health sciences and providing good mentorship for students and young graduates.He is a role model in pharmaceutical and academic spaces..
